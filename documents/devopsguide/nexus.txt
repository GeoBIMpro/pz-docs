## Nexus

* We store build artifacts in https://nexus.devops.geointservices.io/[Nexus].
* Accessing locally - configure maven:

[source,xml]
.settings.xml
----
    <?xml version="1.0"?>
    <settings>
      <servers>
        <server>
          <id>nexus</id>
          <username>YOUR_LDAP_USERNAME</username>
          <password>YOUR_LDAP_PASSWORD</password>
        </server>
      </servers>
    </settings>
----
